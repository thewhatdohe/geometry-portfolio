Key Ideas:

  • Point: location, no size, named with a capital letter (A).
  • Line: infinite, two directions, named by two points (AB) or a lowercase letter (â„“).
  • Plane: flat, infinite surface, named by three non-collinear points (Plane ABC).
  • Collinear Points: points on same line.
  • Coplanar Points: points on same plane.
Points:
  • What is it?: An undefined term, a location.
  • What is it like?: A dot with no dimension.
  • How is it named?: Point A or just "A".


Lines:
  • What is it?: Undefined term.
  • What is it like?: No thickness, goes forever, straight, one dimensional.
  • How is it named?: m, AB, BA (with a line symbol on top).


Planes:
  • What is it?: Undefined term.
  • What is it like?: Flat, extends infinitely.
  • How is it named?: Plane ABC (3 points not on same line).
